Satnav

Over the years i collected millions of locations using a series of satnav receivers. My map shows waypoints, trackpoints and number of trackpoints per hexagon. My hexagons in Plate Carree look afwul in Web Mercator, apologies. Tracks are color coded by average speed, blue is slow and red is fast

Beidou

Beidou is the chinese satnav system. The map shows tha maximum number of satellites observed in a hexagon in colour. The number in the hexagon is the mean number of Beidou satellites. The map is based on one million combined GPS-Beidou locations recorded over a year.
